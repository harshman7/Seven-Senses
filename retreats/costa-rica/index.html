Nicoya Blue

Rainforest, ocean, and long horizons.

expand_more

"Where the Pacific exhales and the canopy holds the rain, something in you remembers how to rest."

On the Nicoya Peninsula, blue water meets dry forest and howler monkeys call at dawn. We design days around tide pools, shaded trails, and the kind of quiet that only arrives when the heat slows everyone down.

Your residence sits between jungle and ocean—glass and teak, salt on the breeze, and hosts who know when to step close and when to disappear. This is tropical luxury as subtraction: fewer plans, more room for your own pulse.
